According to MuseumHack.com, as of 2023 Chicago had 67 museums. Chicago is a city full of interesting places, and if you have an interest there's probably a museum for you. On this website, we will only be going over the museums located on the Museum Campus, museums in Hyde Park, and (of course) the iconic Art Institute.

The Museum Campus is located in Grant Park on the coast of Lake Michigan. This park includes the Adler Planetarium and the Field Museum of National History. Soldier Field and McCormick Place are also located on the Museum Campus. Soldier Field and the Shedd Aquarium are also found on the Museum Campus. Hyde Park is a well-known Chicago neighborhood with many, many cultural institutions. The 4 major museums located here are the DuSable Museum of African American History, the Museum Science and Industry, and Oriental Institute, and the Smart Museum of Art. Among iconic Chicago museums is also the Art Institute of Chicago, which is located in the Loop.

Many of the following museums offer discounts for Illinois and Chicago residents. The ticket prices reflected below are the prices for non-residents. In addition to resident discounts, many museums in Chicago also have free days throughout the year for Illinois residents and partner with Museums for All, a program that provides discounted admission for people with SNAP EBT benefits.
